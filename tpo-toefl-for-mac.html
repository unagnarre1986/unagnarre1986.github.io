TOEFL Writing Topics For Agree&Disagree Type Of Questions

  1. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? Nowadays, it is not possible that young people teach something to older people. Use specific reasons and examples to support your position.
  2. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People act differently based on how they dress. Do you agree that different clothes influence the way people behave? Use specific examples to support your answer.
  3. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People who make more money are more successful. Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  4. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? The best tutors are our own parents. Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  5. Growing up in a big city is more advantageous than in countryside. Do you agree or disagree? Use specific reasons and examples to develop your essay.
  6. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People pay too much attention to the things that are trivial in life and fail to see the larger problems. Use specific reasons and details to explain your opinion.
  7. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People are never satisfied with what they already have; they always go after something they don’t have. Use specific reasons to support your answer.
  8. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? It is more important for students to study literature and history than it is for them to study mathematics and science. Use specific reasons and examples to support your opinion.
  9. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? All pupils should be required to study music and art in high school. Use specific reasons to support your answer.
  10. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? Reading books based on a true story is more enjoyable than watching science related movies. Use specific reasons and examples to explain your position.
  11. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? Childhood years are the most important years for education. Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  12. Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? Playing a game is like a competition and only way to enjoy it is to beat the opponent. Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.

TOEFL Writing Topic For Preference Type Of Questions

  1. Some people like to work more manually, Others prefer using computers. Which do you prefer? Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  2. For some people small towns are more preferable. Others like to live in a big city. Which place would you prefer to live in? Use specific reasons and details to support your answer.
  3. What are the responsibilities of being a parent? Use specific details and examples to explain your answer.
  4. Some people choose friends who have the similar characters like themselves. Others choose friends who are totally different to themselves. Compare the advantages of having friends who are different from you with the advantages of having friends who are similar to you. Which kind of friend do you prefer for yourself? Why?
  5. Which do you feel is more important in your life: being financially independent but with some stress or being dependent someone but stress-free? Use reasons and specific examples to support your opinion.
  6. Some people believe that it is important to have internet with all of these data available. Others think being able to access so much information creates problems. Which view do you agree with? Use specific reasons and examples to support your opinion.
  7. If you are to choose between high speed internet connection but with a higher price or low speed of internet with lower price, which one would you choose and why?
  8. You have 1 day to prepare for a difficult exam. You can choose to take the test on the scheduled day or to postpone it to the next semester. Which one would you choose and why?
  9. Some people are more prone to send text messages than a direct call. Which one would you prefer and why?
  10. Some people do their homework immediately after it is given. Others wait until the due date. Which one do you prefer and why?
  11. Some people new clothes from shopping malls. Others would like to buy used clothes. Which one would you prefer and why?
  12. Some people decide to become who they want as a kid. Others decide before going to the university. Which one would you prefer and why?
  13. Some people watch television. Others watch videos online. Which one would you prefer and why?
  14. Some people work in big companies. Others run their own business. Which one would you prefer and why?

TOEFL Writing Topics For Description / Explanation Type Of Questions

  1. A foreign visitor is going to spend one day in your country. Which tourist attractions should this visitor go on that day? Why? Use specific reasons and details to support your choice.
  2. If you had a chance to pick a time and place in your past, when and where would you go? Why? Use specific reasons and details to support your choice.
  3. What is he one important skill that everyone needs nowadays? Choose one skill and use specific reasons and examples to support your choice.
  4. Why do you think some people are more prone to go for dangerous activities or sports? Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  5. What are some of the crucial responsibilities of a high school teacher? Use specific details and examples to explain why these qualities are important.
  6. If you were asked to introduce an important person representing your country in an international exhibition, what would you choose? Why? Use specific reasons and details to explain your choice.
  7. Describe a custom from your country that it would be better if people from other countries adopt it. Explain your choice, using specific reasons and examples.
  8. There will be a decision on an important person in your country to build his/her statue. Who would you choose? Use reasons and specific examples to support your choice.
  9. Which discovery from the last 50 years have been the most useful in the world? Use specific reasons and examples to support your choice.
  10. Many people at some point in their lives have to live with roommates. What are some of the important qualities of a good roommate? Use specific reasons and examples to explain why these qualities are important.
  11. People attend college or university for many different reasons (for example, new experiences, career preparation, increased knowledge). Why do you think people attend college or university? Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.
  12. A company has announced that it wishes to invest on a lot of money to construct a new company near where you live. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of this new influence on your community. Do you support or oppose the factory? Explain your position.
