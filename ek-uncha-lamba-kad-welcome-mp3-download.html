Nadiadwala and Ronnie Screwvala under the banner of UTV Motion Pictures and Base Industries Group.[3] The film features a large ensemble cast of Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Feroz Khan, Anil Kapoor, Nana Patekar, Paresh Rawal, Mallika Sherawat[4]. Box office[edit]

At the box office, Welcome opened to a massive response locally, by grossing ₹350 million (US$5.1 million) on its opening week.[16] Despite facing competition from the popular Aamir Khan-starrer, Taare Zameen Par which was also a huge success, Welcome grossed ₹700 million (US$10 million) domestically.[17] It became the second highest-grossing film of 2007, just behind Om Shanti Om. The worldwide gross was around ₹1.20 billion (US$17 million).[2]
